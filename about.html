Hey! I'm Dark, and welcome to my Guitar Hero shop.

I have been playing Rhythm Games since 2009, I think my first game was Rock Band Unplugged for the PSP. I never really understood the mechanics but atleast I had fun with it.

It was only when I was introduced to mobile gaming around 2010 where I was able to play games. Then, my first official rhythm game in my mobile device was VOEZ, which was around 2016. Since then, I've been hooked on the rhythm game genre.

But it would only get better around 2017-2018 when I got my first PC. My first game downloaded was Ultimate Custom Night on Steam, and Animal Crossing: Wild World as a ROM. My first rhythm game is this PC was Clone Hero where I downloaded in 2018. I've only been using my fingers like I was playing osu!mania, but eventually around 2021, I bought my first Guitar Hero guitar for as low as $14.

Over the past years, I've been modifying that controller, and now I am proud to share my expertise to you.

I hope my guitars give you satisfaction Guitar Hero and Rock band can't give you.
